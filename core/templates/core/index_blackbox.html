Black Box Reflection

Something that went well during our investigation is our focus, my group was on task and focused on what needed to be completed that day. Something that was a struggle was leakage. While testing our model of the Black box we experience a lot of water leakage. This was not an easy fix, it took lots of flex tape and glue and in the end it still leaked but we realized its a normal part of the process and it was only a prototype.

Claim

There are 2 tubes inside the box that split into different containers.

Evidence

The mystery black box is a container where we don't know what's happening inside. My initial observations of it was that when you pour the water in there is a big delay until it comes out. At first I thought that there were 3 buckets and the tube got higher in each one would need more water which would explain why more came out some trails then others.

How I created my MAYA model

First I started with a regular rectangle in MAYA and then extruded inwards to create a hollow box. Then I added all the other componts in MAYA. After it was done on MAYA I brought it over to merge.edu and then adobe and was able to make a augmented reality.

Models are representations of your ideas. They are very useful when trying to express how your idea works or to even test your theory. There strengths are it can help you prove a theory there limitations are they can also prove your theory incorrect.
